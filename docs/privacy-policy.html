Privacy Policy

Revive Fitness App  ·  Last updated: 15 May 2026  ·  Effective: 15 May 2026

Revive ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness application available on Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Revive, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights over it. Please read it carefully before using the app.

By creating an account or using any part of Revive, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account details — name, email address, password (stored as a one-way hash).
  • Body stats — date of birth, biological sex, height, current and target weight, body fat percentage, BMI.
  • Fitness goals — goal type (lose weight, build muscle, maintain, endurance) and activity level.
  • Food logs — meals and snacks you add, including food name, serving size, and macro-nutrient breakdown.
  • Workout logs — exercises performed, sets, reps, weight lifted, duration, GPS route, and perceived effort.
  • Mood & wellness entries — optional daily mood rating (1–5 emoji scale).
  • Profile photo — if you choose to add one.
  • Support messages — if you contact us by email.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers — Firebase installation ID, advertising ID (used for ad personalisation if consent is given).
  • Usage analytics — screens viewed, features used, session length, button taps (via Firebase Analytics and Amplitude). No individual food or workout content is sent to analytics providers.
  • Crash reports — stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry when the app crashes. These reports do not contain health data.
  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging token generated on first launch, used only to deliver reminders you have opted into.

1.3 Information From Your Device Hardware

  • Camera — used to scan food barcodes, photograph meals for AI nutritional analysis, and stream live video for the AI Exercise Form Monitor. Camera frames are processed on-device and are never stored or transmitted.
  • Photo library — accessed only when you explicitly choose a photo to log a meal. The selected image is sent to Google Gemini for nutritional analysis and then discarded; it is not stored on our servers.
  • Location — collected only during an active outdoor workout session when you tap "Start GPS workout". GPS coordinates are used to draw your route and calculate distance/pace. Raw coordinate streams are discarded after the session is saved; only the aggregate route polyline and distance are stored.
  • Step count — read from your device's built-in pedometer (Android: Google Fit platform; iOS: CoreMotion/HealthKit) to populate the steps tile on your dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalise the Revive service (calorie targets, workout recommendations, AI coach responses).
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (workout reminders, streak warnings, weekly summaries).
  • Power AI features via Google Gemini API — your fitness context (goals, recent logs, body stats) is sent to Gemini to generate meal plans, workout suggestions, and coach replies. Gemini processes this data under Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Display relevant ads via Google AdMob. Ad personalisation requires your consent (shown on first launch). You may opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy.
  • Monitor app stability via Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry.
  • Understand feature usage in aggregate via Firebase Analytics and Amplitude to improve the app.
  • Process subscription payments via Google Play Billing.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account data and fitness logs are retained for as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely (it cannot be linked back to you).
  • Crash report data is retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry.

6. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+.
  • All data at rest on our servers (Google Cloud SQL) is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via flutter_secure_storage.
  • We use short-lived JWT access tokens (15 minutes) and long-lived refresh tokens (30 days) to minimise exposure if a token is compromised.
  • No payment card data is stored on our servers. All payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing.

7. Children's Privacy

Revive is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your account and all associated data. You can initiate this from the app at Settings → Profile → Delete Account, or by emailing us.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON export available on request).
  • Restriction / Objection — object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw any consent you have given (e.g., for personalised ads or health data) at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Lodge a complaint — with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, your national DPA in the EU).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@revivefit.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, to opt out of its "sale" (we do not sell personal information), and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. To submit a request, email privacy@revivefit.app.

10. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the United States (Google Cloud, us-central1 region). If you are located outside the US, your data is transferred to and processed in the US. For EEA/UK users, these transfers are made under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.
